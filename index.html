01 — About

Why companies hire me.

On governance

The most dangerous thing about AI coding isn't that it fails, it's that it succeeds confidently in the wrong direction. That's why I built Atomic Spec: to make AI development predictable, auditable, and safe.

On leverage

Using AI to build software isn't cheating, it's leverage. The value isn't in memorizing syntax. It's in knowing what to build, how to architect it, and when the AI is wrong. That's engineering.

On shipping

Demos are easy. Production is a different sport, auth, rate limits, eval harnesses, rollback paths, the 3 a.m. page. I build for the sport, not the demo.

I'm a full-stack AI engineer specializing in production-grade Generative AI applications. I design RAG pipelines, multi-node agent architectures, and observability systems using LangChain, LangGraph, FastAPI, and modern cloud infrastructure.

AI-assisted development is the future, but only when it's governed, structured, and predictable. That's why I build the guardrails, not just the features.
